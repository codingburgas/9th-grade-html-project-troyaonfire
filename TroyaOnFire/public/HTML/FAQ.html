How can I join the firefighter community?
You can register by clicking on the "Get Started" button and filling in a brief profile with your location and interests.
How do I report a fire incident?
Use the "Fire Map" page to quickly mark and report incidents directly to the community and local authorities.
Can I share fire safety tips?
Absolutely! In the "Community" section, you can share tips, post educational content, and engage with other users.
Is this platform official?
While community-driven, the platform works in collaboration with certified firefighters and emergency services in several regions.
